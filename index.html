Cineplexity Clone

Fast rounds, big clues, easy scorekeeping.

Load your deck from cards.csv, add your players, and run the whole game from one simple screen while the clues stay readable on a mirrored TV.

Two clue cards stay on screen at once, with the right card carrying forward into the next round.
Tap the winning player to score the round and advance the deck instantly.
Edit your deck in a spreadsheet-friendly CSV without touching the app code.

Players

Enter at least two players. Blank rows are ignored, and you can add more as needed.

Game Options

Default game length is five points, but you can change it before starting.
